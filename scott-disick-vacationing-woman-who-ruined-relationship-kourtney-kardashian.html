Scott Disick Seen Vacationing With Woman Who Ruined His Relationship With Kourtney Kardashian

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Highlights

  • Scott Disick tries to distract himself from Kourtney Kardashian's new marriage with Travis Barker by vacationing with Chloe Bartoli.
  • Photos of Scott and Chloe together raise eyebrows due to their past history and the fact that Chloe allegedly contributed to Scott and Kourtney's breakup in 2015.
  • Kourtney has moved on and is happy with Travis, leaving Scott feeling left out and struggling to see her marry someone else. His getaway with Chloe may indicate an attempt at moving on.

Scott Disick has reportedly had a hard time seeing Kourtney Kardashian move on in her marriage with Travis Barker, who she welcomed a baby boy with last month. But it seems Scott is now trying to distract himself with a familiar face – stylist Chloe Bartoli.

Scott and Chloe were seen getting close while in St. Barts over the holidays. Paparazzi photos showed the exes frolicking in the water as well as grabbing beverages at a beachside bar

Scott Vacationed With The Girl He Cheated On Kourtney With

Chloe was dressed in a pink and black patterned bikini. She threw a crochet dress over the swimsuit when out of the water. For his part, Scott looked casual in dark green swim trunks with a beige shirt worn over top.

As of now, it’s not known if Scott and Chloe are officially together. The duo have been infamously on-and-off in the past, though they haven’t been linked in nearly a decade.

Scott and Chloe’s romance isn’t only raising eyebrows amongst fans because of their age difference, but also because of their history. Chloe was allegedly part of the reason Kourtney broke up with Scott for good in 2015, shortly after they welcomed their third child, son Reign.

Photos published in July 2015 showed Scott and Chloe together in Monte Carlo, while Kourtney presumably was home with their newborn. Moreover, Scott had allegedly been unfaithful in the past, a topic that explored on Keeping Up on the Kardashians numerous times.

Scott did later address their break-up in the show, admitting his actions were to blame. "I made the worst decision I've ever made in my life," he said.

Scott Struggled Seeing Kourtney Move On With Travis Barker

Scott and Kourtney also had an on-and-off relationship for years, so there was speculation they would reunite, even after he seemed to step out on her with Chloe. However, that never happened.

"Kourtney is 100% over Scott," a source told Entertainment Tonight in 2016. "Not in a hateful way at all, but she has completely moved on."

"She is the happiest she's been in a really long time -- since probably before Mason was born," they continued. "When she had him, she really became introverted and only wanted to stay home with the kids."

The insider added, "This breakup has really showed her that she needs to focus on herself. She's spending a lot of time with her girlfriends, is planning dinners and nights out. She's happy and glowing."

Kourtney began dating Blink 182 drummer Travis Barker in 2021 and they married last year in multiple ceremonies, including a star-studded one in Italy. She gave birth to their son, Rocky, in November after revealing she used IVF treatments.

Scott has expressed feeling left out of the Kardashian-Jenner family since Kourtney and Travis began dating, while reports say he’s struggled seeing her marry someone else (though he proposed several times, Kourtney never accepted Scott’s hand in marriage during their relationship). But maybe Scott’s recent getaway with Chloe is a sign he’s trying to move on, too.
