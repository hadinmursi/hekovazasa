Evangeline Lilly Slams Her &quot;Best Butt Ever&quot; Women's Health Cover

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Butt seriously… Evangeline Lilly didn’t really appreciate her “Best Butt Ever” cover of Women’s Health‘s upcoming January/February 2014 issue.

The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug star, 34, stopped by Conan O’Brien‘s Conan show on Wednesday, Dec. 4 and admitted that she was unhappy with the mag.

O’Brien, who broached the topic with Lilly, gave a brief description of the fitness magazine’s cover: “They go, ‘Best. Butt. Ever.’ and they have an arrow going to your butt,” he said, holding up the issue in question. “I’m just curious — is that something they tell you before you do the shoot?” 

Lilly retorted, “No!” The mom to 2-year-old son Kahekili asked, “Do you think I would have done the shoot if they said, ‘Oh, by the way, we’re gonna say ‘Best Butt Ever!’ and put a big arrow to your behind,'” as she signaled to her posterior. “You know, every woman is after a kind of classy image,” she explained. “I didn’t want T&A on my cover. It’s a women’s magazine, not Maxim.”

The former Lost actress added that she later spoke to the magazine to express her displeasure over the butt/arrow addition. Lilly is not alone, however, in all this. Chris Hemsworth‘s hot (and pregnant again) wife Elsa Pataky was featured on Women’s Health‘s June 2013 cover — with the words “Great Butt!” and an arrow pointed to her sculpted backside.

The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug — in which Lilly plays an elf named Tauriel — hits theaters Dec. 13.
