Top 10 Richest Rappers in Ghana &amp; Net Worth

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Ghanaian rappers have represented the rapping game with great talents and skill. Popular rappers in the Ghanaian music industry include Sarkodie, M.anifest, Teephlow, and some others. These are popular names in the industry who have gathered popularity and a huge fanbase overtime.

These rappers have also accumulated wealth from their album sales, labels, shows, music tours, and endorsement deals. This is a list of the top 10 richest rappers in the Ghanaian music industry and their net worth.

Richest Rappers in Ghana

1. Sarkodie

Sarkodie tops the list of richest rappers in the Ghanaian music industry. He is known as the rap God in the industry. He has won awards which include BET Awards, with the help of his skill in rapping. Sarkodie has gathered enough wealth from his rap business to make his way to the top of the list of richest rappers in Ghana.

2. Manifest

Number 2 on this list of Ghana’s richest rappers is M.anifest. This rapper has made his mark on the music industry. He has released a series of songs including his hit song titled god MC. He was also nominated at VGMA 2019 for Best Rapper of the Year. M.anifest as made his way to being the 2nd richest rapper in Ghana.

3. Teephlow

He got nominated at VGMA 2019 for the award of Best Rapper. Teephlow has had a lot of feats in the Ghanaian music industry and has reached out to a lot of music lovers and fans. He is on the 3rd spot on this list.

4. Eno Barony

Her real name is Ruth Eno Adjoa Amankwah Nyame Adom and she is the only female in this list, she was also nominated for Best Rapper award at VGMA 2019. She is currently the 4th richest rapper in Ghana and the richest female rapper in the country’s music industry.

5. Obibini

Obibini has grown to be one of the most popular music artists in Nigeria. The rapper has a huge fanbase comprising music lovers in the country and internationally. He is known to be one of the best rappers in the country. Hence it is no surprise that he is 5th on this list.

6. Kwesi Arthur

This Ghanaian rapper is popularly for his mix of old and new music. he has a large fan base of music lovers in and outside the country. He is currently 6th on the list of richest rappers in the country.

7. AMG Medikal

AMG Medikal currently occupies the 7th spot on this list of Ghana’s richest rappers. He won the 2019 VGMA award for Best Rapper.

8. Strongman Burner

Also known as “King of gods”, Strongman Burner is 8th on the list. He is one of the most popular music artists in the country and he has a considerably large fanbase who love and admire his music.

9. Tulenkey

Tulenkey is another popular and talented rapper in the Ghanaian music industry. He has made a series of hit songs including Proud Fvck Boy. He was selected as one of Sarkodie’s best rappers for the track titled Biibi Ba.

10. Amerado

Amerado is one of the rappers in Ghana who are still on the rise in their career. He was also selected by Sarkodie for the track, Biibi Ba.

Conclusion

The list of top richest rappers in Ghana is ever-changing with time. This means that the rappers have the opportunity to rise in the ranks. Let’s hear your thoughts and comments in the comments section.
