Did Shakira And Gisele Bndchen Confide In Each Other After Their Extremely Public Divorces?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Highlights

  • Shakira and Gisele Bündchen have recently formed a surprising friendship after their high-profile breakups from their athlete partners.
  • Both Shakira and Gisele have expressed their feelings about their breakups publicly, with Shakira releasing songs that seemingly address her breakup, and Gisele talking about her divorce in interviews.
  • The dinner gathering between Shakira and Gisele, along with their children, indicates that they have developed a strong bond and are finding support and camaraderie in each other's company as they navigate single motherhood.

Both Shakira and Gisele Bündchen are household names in the entertainment industry. Shakira is a highly successful and iconic Latin singer dubbed the "Queen of Latin Music" and Gisele's incredibly impressive modeling career stands out as one of the most successful of its kind. Both superstars have ignited headlines after their high-profile romances and drama-fueled breakups.

In October 2022, TMZ reported that Gisele and football superstar Tom Brady were ending their 13-year marriage. Shakira and soccer player Gerard Piqué announced that they were splitting up in June 2022 after rumors of infidelity.

In May 2023, Shakira and Gisele were pictured hanging out together in Miami, fans were surprised by their sudden friendship with many people speculating that the pair struck up a friendship as a result of their mutual break-ups from both athletes. Many fans are curious about the truth behind this unexpected friendship...

What Did Gisele Bundchen Say About Divorcing Tom Brady?

In October 2022, news broke that Tom Brady and Gisele Bundchen were getting a divorce in Florida, where Tom plays for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. An insider told People "The settlement is all worked out, they’ve been working on the terms this whole time." They also agreed to share custody of their kids. Both of them even posted about it on their social media accounts.

Related: What Happened Between Tiger Woods And Gisele Bundchen?

Tom confirmed the divorce on his Instagram Story. He wrote, "Recently, my wife and I finalized our divorce after 13 years of marriage. We decided to do this on good terms and with gratitude for the time we had together. Our kids are really important to us, and we'll keep working together as parents to make sure they get the love and care they deserve."

Gisele shared a similar message on her Instagram, she wrote, “With much gratitude for our time together, Tom and I have amicably finalized our divorce. My priority has always been and will continue to be my children whom I love with all my heart. We will continue co-parenting to give them love, care and attention they need. The decision to end a marriage is never easy, but we have grown apart and while it is, of course, difficult to go through something like this, I feel blessed for the time we had together and only wish the best for Tom always.”

Before all this, there were rumors in October 2022 that they hired lawyers to help with the divorce. Someone reported to Page Six, "Things are pretty nasty between Tom and Gisele because of the lawyers. They're ready to fight." There were also stories that even though they were putting on a civil front in public, they were actually having a tough time behind the scenes.

In March 2023, Gisele finally talked about the divorce. She said, "It feels like a part of me has died, but also like a new beginning." She talked about how tough it is when things don't turn out the way you hoped, even though you tried your best.

She said, "I believed in fairy tales when I was a kid. It's a beautiful thing to believe in. I'm really glad I did." She added, "You put everything into chasing your dream. You give it your all, and it's really sad when it doesn't work out the way you wanted, even though you did everything you could."

Shakira Released Shady Songs After Toxic Break-up From Gerard Piqué

Shakira has never held back when it comes to expressing her feelings about her former long-term partner Gerard Piqué through her music. In a joint statement shared with E! News, they expressed, "We want to confirm our decision to part ways. Our priority is the well-being of our children, and we ask for respect for their privacy."

In a candid conversation with Elle, Shakira opened up about the breakup, she revealed, “Talking about this is really tough... especially since I'm still experiencing it, and it's not a typical separation. It has been incredibly hard, not just for me, but also for my children. I can only say that I gave my all to this relationship and my family.”

Related: Recent Rumors Of Shakira And Pique Being In An Open Relationship Have Been Shut Down By The Singer

While fans speculated about infidelity on Gerard's part, neither party explicitly confirmed nor denied these rumors. Shakira subtly addressed the situation through lyrics that seemed to allude to Gerard allegedly cheating on her.

In her song "Te Felicito (I Congratulate You)," released last April, she sang in Spanish, “To make you whole / I broke myself in pieces / I was warned, but I didn't take heed / Don't tell me you're sorry / I know you well, and I know you’re lying.” When asked if these words were about her relationship, Shakira responded, “Everything I feel, everything I go through is reflected in the lyrics I write, in the videos I make.”

In her song, "BZRP Music Sessions #53," a collaboration with DJ Bizarrap, she seemed to throw more shade "I’ve outgrown you and that's why you're with a girl just like you / I'm not coming back to you, even if you're crying or begging."

Are Shakira And Gisele Bundchen Friends After Their Breakups?

Shakira and Gisele Bundchen were seen dining together, they were pictured on Tuesday evening in relaxed yet stylish outfits at Makoto restaurant in Miami Beach, Florida. They shared a cozy dinner with their kids, including Shakira's sons Milan, 10, and Sasha, 8, and Gisele's children, son Benjamin, 13, and daughter Vivian, 10.

This heartwarming gathering left many fans wondering about the origins of their friendship. Laughter and joyful moments filled the air as they savored rolls, hamachi and salads.

Shakira and Gisele Bündchen are both fresh from the ends of significant relationships, and seem to have developed a strong bond, uniting their families for a memorable dinner date.

It's clear that Shakira and Gisele have transitioned into single motherhood after parting ways with their high-profile athlete partners last year. Sources speculate that the pair are potentially finding camaraderie in each other's company.

They suggest that their friendship may have deepened in the wake of their recent separations.

Gisele had nothing but admiration for Shakira, sharing her sentiments with Quien magazine, "She is a genuine, heartfelt, strong person, yet wonderfully down-to-earth. I'm Shakira's number one fan." The Brazilian beauty expressed her fondness for Shakira's music.

Their journey together traces back to 2010, during the FIFA World Cup in South Africa, a momentous occasion marked by Spain's inaugural victory. Since that meeting, their connection has seems to have only grown stronger.
