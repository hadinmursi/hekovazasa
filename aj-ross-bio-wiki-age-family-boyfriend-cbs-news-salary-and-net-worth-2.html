AJ Ross Career

AJ began her career in the field of Journalism. In January 2006 she worked as a Production Assistant for two years. After two years she left the job and worked with WMBF. She worked with them for two years as a News Anchor/ Reporter. In December 2010 she worked for WSYX as a News Anchor/ Reporter in Columbus, Ohio area. In 2013 she worked for 3 months as a freelance reporter with WTAE. The same year she began working for WKYC- TV as a News Reporter. Next year she worked for WABC as a News Reporter for 4 years. In February 2018 she started working as a Sports Reporter for CBS Sports. She has worked with them for more than 3 years in the Greater New York City area.
