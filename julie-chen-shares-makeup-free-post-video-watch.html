Julie Chen Shares Makeup-Free Post, Video: Watch

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Natural beauty! Julie Chen stunned in a makeup-free video she shared on Friday, September 11, and we can’t get enough.

The 50-year-old T.V. personality took to Twitter to talk about Forgiveness Friday bare-faced and seriously beautiful. The Big Brother host opted out of her usual done-up, glam makeup and instead kept it au naturel for the nearly 2-minute clip.

“Never saw Julie without makeup,” commented one user.

While it is rare, it’s not the first time Chen’s kept it fresh-faced. In fact, over the past couple weeks on Sunday’s she’s made it a routine to share the sermon from Reverend Lee Boddie in makeup-free Instagram videos under the hashtag #SharingSunday.

As much as we admire a fierce makeup look, we truly love when celebs embrace their natural beauty, from Gisele Bundchen’s flawless selfies to Kristen Bell’s glowing pics.

Chen’s always been one to keep it real, though. Back in 2013, she revealed on The Talk that she got work done to make her “Asian eyes” look bigger after receiving racist feedback from a boss.

“I was working as a local news reporter in Dayton, Ohio,” she said at the time. “I asked my news director over the holidays, ‘If anchors want to take vacations, could I fill in?’ And he said, ‘You will never be on this anchor desk, because you’re Chinese.” She continued: “He said, ‘Let’s face it, Julie, how relatable are you to our community? How big of an Asian community do we have in Dayton?’ On top of that, because of your heritage, because of your Asian eyes, sometimes I’ve noticed when you’re on camera and you’re interviewing someone, you look disinterested, you look bored.'”
