Mackenzie Dern Divorce And Settlement: New Boyfriend And Relationship

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Explore the aftermath of Mackenzie Dern divorce as she candidly shares the challenges and impact on her life ahead of UFC 295.

Mackenzie Dern is a Brazilian-American mixed martial artist and former world champion Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioner.

She made her mark in the martial arts with numerous Jiu-Jitsu championships before transitioning to professional MMA.

Competing in the strawweight division of the UFC, Dern is known for her exceptional grappling skills and has become a rising star in the women’s MMA scene.

Details About Mackenzie Dern Divorce And Settlement

Mackenzie Dern, the accomplished Brazilian-American mixed martial artist, was in the spotlight due to her highly publicized divorce and subsequent settlement with Wesley Santos. 

The martial arts sensation was embroiled in a tumultuous divorce with her ex-husband, which unfolded before her clash with Angela Hill in May.

The divorce proceedings, which included a custody battle for their daughter, added an extra layer of complexity to Dern’s already demanding professional life.

Despite finalizing the divorce, Dern revealed the lingering aftermath, stating that the financial aspects of the settlement were still impacting her, even during her preparations for UFC 295.

The admission sheds light on the enduring challenges accompanying legal separations, extending beyond the courtroom into various aspects of one’s life.

In addition to the personal turmoil, Dern faced the closure of her gym, RVCA, further disrupting her routine.

Undeterred, she showcased her resilience by creating a private training facility in her home, underlining her commitment to her athletic aspirations.

To add a unique dynamic to her life, Dern introduced a new element by getting a puppy, acknowledging the chaos and challenges that come with it.

This decision reflects her ability to thrive in the midst of adversity, embracing the unpredictability of life both inside and outside the Octagon.

Despite the personal struggles, Dern remained steadfast in her commitment to her career, refusing to use her challenges as excuses.

She acknowledged that other fighters, including her UFC 295 opponent Jessica Andrade, also grapple with personal issues, highlighting the shared experiences within the fighting community.

Who Is Mackenzie Dern New Boyfriend? Relationship Timeline

Mackenzie Dern’s new boyfriend is Antonio Trócoli, also known as “Malvado.” He is a 32-year-old mixed martial artist from Brazil with a record of 12-3-0, 1 NC.

The relationship between Dern and Trócoli marks a new chapter in her personal life after a highly publicized divorce from pro surfer Wesley Santos.

Antonio Trócoli has had a tumultuous journey in the MMA world, which includes a failed test for nandrolone and a release from the UFC after being pulled from the lineup.

Despite these setbacks, he has shown resilience and continued his career, last competing in the Brazilian Fighting Series in November 2021.

The couple’s relationship progressed to the point where they adopted a cane corso puppy, signifying a shared commitment to building a life together.

This addition to their family reflects their personal connection and readiness to embrace responsibilities together.

Beyond their personal life, Mackenzie and Antonio have shared experiences in the world of mixed martial arts.

They visited Henry Cejudo’s gym together, showcasing a mutual interest in training and further solidifying their connection within the MMA community.

As Mackenzie Dern continues to navigate her personal and professional life, her relationship with Antonio Trócoli appears to be a supportive and integral part of her journey.
