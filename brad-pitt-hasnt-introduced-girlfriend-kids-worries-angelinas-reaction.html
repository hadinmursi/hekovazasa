Brad Pitt Still Hasnt Introduced Girlfriend To Kids As He Worries About Angelinas Reaction

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 12, 2024

Highlights

  • Brad Pitt is hesitant to introduce his girlfriend to his children out of fear of how Angelina Jolie will react.
  • Angelina Jolie plans to drag out the ongoing custody battle until their children are adults.
  • Brad Pitt's relationship with his kids appears strained, as he hasn't been seen with them in public for a while and they skipped his 60th birthday celebrations.

There have been reports that Brad Pitt and his girlfriend Ines De Ramon want to start a family together. Even though they appear to be getting serious, a new report says Ines hasn’t met all of Brad’s six kids as he fears Angelina Jolie’s response.

"Brad's walking on eggshells when it comes to introducing his children to his new girlfriend," a source told National Enquirer. "The caution stems from a desire to avoid additional tensions with Angie."

Brad Pitt Is Afraid To Introduce His Kids To Ines De Ramon

"She's not just the kids' mother, she's their best friend,” they continued. "Can you imagine what a meeting would be like between the kids and Ines? No wonder he's in no rush to make the introductions!"

Brad is still engaged in an ongoing custody battle with Angelina over their minor children. Even though he’s reportedly spent millions on the fight, one report says Angie is unwilling to drop the case and plans to drag it out until all their kids are over 18.

“She is going to drag this [custody battle] out until the twins [now 14] turn 18,” a source said last year. “Four more years.”

The Kids Spend Most Of Their Time With Angelina

Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt got together in 2004 after meeting on the set of the action-comedy film Mr. & Mrs. Smith. The Hollywood power couple tied the knot in a private ceremony at their estate in France on August 23, 2014.

Unfortunately, their marriage came to an end in September 2016 when Jolie filed for divorce, citing irreconcilable differences. Angie’s decision came after an alleged incident on a private jet in which Brad got physical with her and several of their kids. The FBI subsequently investigated the situation but cleared Brad of any wrongdoing. The divorce was finalized on April 12, 2019.

During their relationship, Jolie and Pitt adopted three children internationally: Maddox from Cambodia in 2002, Pax from Vietnam in 2007, and Zahara from Ethiopia in 2005. They also have three biological children: Shiloh, born in Namibia in 2006, and twins Knox and Vivienne, born in France in 2008.

Brad hasn’t been seen in public with his kids in a record amount of time. Conversely, Angelina is routinely seen with her kids in tow. Last year, she revealed that she’d hired their youngest daughter Vivienne as a production assistant for her off-Broadway Outsiders show. One report says Brad believes Angelina was mainly motivated to involve Vivienne in the show simply to drive an even bigger wedge between the teen and her dad.
