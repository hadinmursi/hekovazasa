Bobby Joseph Wagner is an American football linebacker for the Los Angeles Rams of the National Football League (NFL).

Bobby Wagner played college football at Utah State, and was drafted by the Seattle Seahawks in the second round of the 2012 NFL Draft and played with them until the end of the 2021 season.

Bob Wagner  high school

Bobby Wagner attended Colony High School in Ontario, California, where he played high school football for the Titans.

In 2007, Bobby Wagner registered 125 tackles (92 solo, 33 assists), including four sacks as a senior.

Bobby Wagner also played tight end, tallying 37 catches for 595 yards with 11 touchdowns and received all-CIF Central Division honors as well as all-Mount Baldy League accolades.

Bob Wagner  college

Bobby Wagner attended Utah State University from 2008 to 2011.

A four-year starter, Bobby Wagner recorded 445 tackles, 4.5 sacks, and four interceptions during his career.

As a senior in 2011, Bobby Wagner was the WAC Defensive Player of the Year after recording 147 tackles, four sacks, and two interceptions.
