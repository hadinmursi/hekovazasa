Charlotte Corden, The Youngest Daughter Of James Corden

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Charlotte Corden is the youngest daughter of James Corden, an English comedian, actor, writer, and television personality. 

Charlotte’s father is well known for hosting the Late Late Show with James Corden, a discussion show that has been broadcast on CBS since 2015.

The Accidental Birth Of Charlotte

Charlotte was born to Corden and his partner Julia Carey. 

In an interview, James Corden revealed that the birth of Charlotte came as a surprise. 

Corden continued that having Charlotte was much more than he and his wife could have imagined. 

Charlotte’s delivery occurred a little bit sooner than anticipated. James and Julia were somewhat surprised when Charlotte was born. 

Charlotte wasn’t scheduled to come for another couple of weeks. 

The Hillingdon-born broadcaster had initially gone for a routine check-up with his then-pregnant wife, but the doctors informed the host and his then-pregnant wife that they might be having their third child shortly.

Although James and his wife, a former TV producer, have three children, including Charlotte, they are pretty private about their personal lives.

The Late Late Show host doesn’t post any pictures of his kids on social media, although he has occasionally expressed how things are going with them. 

The paternal ancestry of Charlotte Corden, born in December 2017, originates in Uttoxeter, Staffordshire, England. 

When James found out his wife was giving birth, he was ready to start hosting the talk show The Late Late Show With James Corden. 

He immediately got in touch with pop singer Harry Styles to take over hosting duties for the show. 

Charlotte joined her older brother Max and older sister Carey after birth.

 Max, her sibling, was born in March 2011. Paul McCartney, a founding Beatles member, gave him his first and middle name.

Carey Corden, her older sister, was born on October 27, 2014. She was given the same name as Julia’s mother. 

Just before the former and his family relocated from the UK to the US so that he could launch The Late Late Show, James and Julia welcomed Carey. 

Cedric Kimberley “Kim” Corden, a musician and the son of Thomas Edward Corden and May Wheeldon, is her paternal great-grandfather. 

Cedric, the great-grandfather of Charlotte, was born in Uttoxeter, Staffordshire. 

The Story Behind Charlotte Corden’s Name

The now 4-year-old Charlotte almost had the name of a well-known Grammy-winning musician, Beyonce, because of her early delivery. 

‘We still haven’t decided on a name for her yet,’ Corden remarked while recalling the moment he almost named his daughter Beyonce in a People interview from December 2017.

 ‘I was crying, my wife was crying uncontrollably, the baby was crying, and the doctor inquired about a name after the baby was born. We’re going to call her Beyoncé,’ I said, ‘looking at the doctor for some reason. That was not amusing in my wife’s opinion.’

Charlotte’s father claims the nurse started filling out papers with Queen Bey’s name despite Charlotte’s father’s claim that she only said the name on a whim. 

Relationship between Chorlette And Her Father

The now-43-year-old Englishman also said that it seemed wrong and dreadful of him to consider wrapping up the program that day when his wife was eight and a half months pregnant, and the doctor was discussing critical issues regarding childbirth with her.

James understandably felt the urge to be at her side and immediately left his job to hurry to the delivery room, where Julia gave birth to their third child, Charlotte. 

James Corden, a celebrity actor, acknowledges that having three young children has been a nightmare,

 James has frequently made jokes about how much of a burden having three children was.

Charlotte’s Father- James Corden

James Corden was born on August 22, 1978, in Hillingdon, London, England. He is the son of Margaret and Malcolm Corden.

James Corden is a British actor, comedian, writer, and producer. He is best known for his role as Smithy in the hit BBC sitcom Gavin & Stacey. 

Corden has starred in several films, including Begin Again and Into the Woods. He currently hosts The Late Late Show with James Corden, a late-night talk show on CBS. 

His mother was a social worker, and his father was a musician. Corden attended HOLY Trinity Church of England Primary School in Hillingdon. He then studied drama at the Jackie Palmer Stage School in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire. 

After graduation, he worked various odd jobs before landing his first professional acting role in the play The History Boys in 2004. 

The play earned him critical acclaim, and he starred in several films and television shows. 

In 2015, Corden began hosting The Late Late Show with James Corden, a popular late-night talk show. In addition to his acting and hosting duties, Corden is also a successful writer, producer, and musician. 

He has won several awards for his work, including three Tony Awards and four Emmy Awards.

The Marriage Of Charlotte Corden’s Parents

Charlotte’s parents, James and Julia, met in 2009 and married in 2012. 

They encountered each other at a charity event since Julia used to work for Save The Children at the time, though now she is primarily a stay-at-home mother.

Shortly after meeting each other, James got straight to the point, and not long after, he and Julia were planning their future. 

Charlotte’s father suggested they instead not go out like a normal couple and stay all week which his wife Carey agreed.

Corden is married to Julia Carey, with whom he has three children named Carey, Max, and Charlotte.   

The family currently resides in Los Angeles, California.
