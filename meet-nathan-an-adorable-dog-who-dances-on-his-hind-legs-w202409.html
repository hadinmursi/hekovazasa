Meet Nathan, an Adorable Dog Who Dances on His Hind Legs!

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 19, 2024

Cue up “Who Let the Dogs Out?”

Nat Geo Wild’s April 15 World’s Funniest Dogs special features Nathan, a frisky canine who likes to cut loose on his hind legs — and Us Weekly has an exclusive clip. “When Nathan dances, his floppy little ears and hair swing back and forth,” owner Tina Hicks says of her adopted hairless Chinese Crested.

According to Hicks, the 9-year-old has two go-to moves. “He has this little, like, two-step thing going on, but he’ll also do a circle and then get back up on his back and do it again.”

His skills have earned him quite the fan following. Nathan’s YouTube page, featuring videos of him dancing to Cupid’s “Cupid Shuffle,” Macklemore’s “Thrift Shop” and Psy’s “Gangnam Style,” has nearly 800,000 followers. His Facebook page has more than 65,000 likes.

But Nathan’s talents aren’t limited to dancing. “He can jump really high, he can smile,” says Hicks. “He’s just very unique in so many different ways, but has a little bit of that comedian in him, too, with his personality.”
