Who Is Brooke Bailey On Basketball Wives? Ronnie Holland Wife And Children Details

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Brooke Bailey is a social media influencer and the ex-wife of former basketball player Ronnie Holland. She makes her return in the new season of the reality television series Basketball Wives. Here are details about her personal life.

Bailey is one of the lead women in the new reality series which premiered on VH1 on May 16, 2022. In the series, a group of women who are currently or used to be in a relationship with a basketball player come together on the show to tell their story.

Who Is Brooke Bailey On Basketball Wives?

Brooke Bailey is a television star most famously known for her appearance in season 2 of the VH1 reality tv series Basketball Wives LA which aired in 2012. She is the ex-wife of Ronnie Holland. She makes her return to the show’s new season which premiered on May 16, 2022.

Brooke is an entrepreneur and a businesswoman who is active in the beauty and fashion field. She is the owner of Skin by Brooke Beauty, a company that sells beauty products related to anti-aging skincare. She sells her products through her website brookebaileyinc.com.

Additionally, Brooke is also the owner of the clothing brand, Opulent Icon. The store sells sportswear and other comfortable clothes. She is also the owner of Brooke Bailey Book Club, where she shares her book recommendations. She is also the owner of My Life Herbs.

Brooke Bailey Age Details

Brooke Bailey is currently 44 years old. She was born on July 13, 1977, in California. She made her Basketball Wives LA debut in 2012 at the age of 35 when she was dating basketball player Vernon Macklin.

The couple broke up and Brooke was romantically linked to another pro basketball player Ronnie Holland. The two married in 2014 in a high-profile ceremony. Though the couple exchanged their vows in 2016, the marriage did not last long, and Brooke separated from Ronnie, as per her Instagram.

Moreover, Brooke was featured on the cover of Low Rider magazine alongside rapper Ice Cube. She also appeared in a commercial alongside Snoop Dogg. She was also featured in several other magazines including Gorgeous Magazine.

Brooke Bailey Children Details

Brooke Bailey is a mother of three children; two sons and a daughter. She features her children on her Instagram quite often.

Brooke’s daughter Kayla Bailey, born in 1996, is a model and has 4400 followers on her Instagram. Her elder son Sumecio, born in 1998, has 5600 followers on his Instagram. Her younger son was born in 2004. The father of the children is not disclosed.

The 44-year-old is on Instagram as @brookebaileyinc. She has 533K followers. Brooke can be seen on Basketball Wives 2022 on VH1.
