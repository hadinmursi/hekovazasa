Biography

Lungelihle Zwane (born March 30, 2000), professionally known as Uncle Waffles, is a celebrated DJ and record producer renowned for her expertise in the amapiano genre.

Her influence in the amapiano genre, coupled with her remarkable dance abilities, has earned her the title of the “princess of amapiano,” as recognized by Billboard.

Beyond her musical prowess, Uncle Waffles is equally celebrated for her impressive dance skills. She has also set a unique fashion trend with her distinctive style, often seen donning oversized sweaters and sneakers, making a fashion statement all her own.

Early Life & Education

Uncle Waffles, a renowned musical genius, was born in Swaziland on March 30, 2000. Her grandmother raised her and met her parents when she was a teenager. Her parents are called Mr. and Mrs. Zwane, but their names remain unknown. 

No information is available about her siblings. After completing high school in Swaziland, Uncle Waffles pursued a degree in information technology at the University of KwaZulu-Natal. Still, she dropped out to follow her passion for creating and performing music on a global scale.

Career

Uncle Waffles, who began her DJing journey in 2020 amidst the COVID-19 lockdowns, acquired her skills through online platforms and dedicated eight hours daily to perfecting her skills. The following year, she joined forces with KreativeKornerr, a South African creative agency, which facilitated her gig bookings and provided her with valuable exposure.

Waffles‘ career skyrocketed in 2022 when a video of her dancing to Young Stunna‘s Adiwele went viral on social media in October. As a result, Canadian rapper Drake started following her on Instagram. Additionally, Waffles achieved further success by releasing her debut EP, Red Dragon, in March 2022. The EP was a commercial hit, with the lead single, Tanzania, becoming a popular sensation.

The year 2023 witnessed an even more significant surge in popularity for Uncle Waffles. Making history, she became the inaugural amapiano artist to grace the stage at the renowned Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival. Simultaneously, her second EP, titled Asylum, was released to immense commercial acclaim, solidifying her position as a rising star in the music industry.

Personal Life

Uncle Waffles, 23, is romantically involved with Thato Tlhankana, known as Kai. What makes their relationship even more interesting is that Kai happens to be the co-founder and manager of Kreative Korner, the management agency that represents Uncle Waffles. 

Their love story began in early 2023, and they have been going strong since then. Although they have been together for some time, the couple has no children. Uncle Waffles has never been married.
