Lorenzo Brino Biography – Lorenzo Brino Wiki

Lorenzo Brino was an American actor. He was best known for his role as Sam Camden on The WB’s 1996 drama 7th Heaven.

7th Heaven told the story of the Camden family of Glenoak, California for 11 seasons. The Camden’s were led by Protestant minister Reverend Eric Camden (Stephen Collins), his wife Annie (Catherine Hicks), and their 7 children played by Jessica Biel, Barry Watson, Beverly Mitchell, David Gallagher, and Mackenzie Rosman, according to EW.

Lorenzo Brino and his brother Nikolas portrayed the youngest of the Camden kids, fraternal twin boys named Sam and David who were born during the show’s third season. The roles were originally played by the Brino quadruplets, including Mimi before Lorenzo and Nikolas took over the roles officially.

Lorenzo Brino Age

Lorenzo Brino was born on September 21, 1998, in Woodland Hills, Los Angeles, California, United States. He died March 9, 2020, in San Bernardino County, California, United States. He was 21 years old.

Lorenzo Brino Parents

Lorenzo Brino’s parents are Tony Brino and Shawna Brino.

Lorenzo Brino Siblings

Lorenzo Brino had 4 siblings; 3 brothers, Antonio Brino, Zachary Brino and Nikolas Landon Brino, and a sister Myrinda Brino.

Lorenzo Brino Family

Lorenzo Brino is survived by his parents, Tony Brino and Shawna Brino, brother Antonio Brino and his quadruplet siblings—brothers Zachary Brino and Nikolas Brino and sister Myrinda “Mimi” Brino.

Lorenzo Brino Death – Lorenzo Brino Car Accident

Lorenzo Brino died in a car accident on March 9, 2020 at the age of 21. He lost control of his 2016 Toyota Camry and collided with a utility pole, according to the San Bernadino County Sheriff’s Department.

The San Bernadino County Sheriff’s Department said in a press release: “On Monday, March 9, 2020, at 3:05 AM, Lorenzo Antonio Brino, a 21-year-old resident of Yucaipa was driving a 2016 Toyota Camry. Brino lost control of the Toyota on Yucaipa Blvd. and 16th St., in Yucaipa and collided with a utility pole. Brino was pronounced dead at the scene. The San Bernardino County Sheriff’s Department Major Accident Investigation Team is investigating the collision.”

Lorenzo Brino’s sister Myrinda “Mimi” Brino paid tribute to Lorenzo in an Instagram post on Wednesday, 11 March 2020. She wrote on Instagram: “To my amazing and crazy brother. You might be gone but it’s true when people say “gone but never forgotten”. Saying that you were a blessing is an understatement. You brought light to so many lives and you did so much with the too short of a life that you had. I had the privilege of growing up side by side with you for an absolutely wild 21 years. Believe me when I say that you drove me so insane at times, but you were also a part of some of my most cherished memories. I am so so happy to know that I was beyond loved by you and that I forever have a guardian angel by my side. Rest In Peace Lorenzo, I love you now and I’ll love you forever.”

Lorenzo’s aunt, Janet Brino, said in a statement to TMZ: “To my dear sweet nephew, your loss left a hole in my heart. God needed another Angel and he took you. Please watch over your mom and dad. You sister Mimi, brothers, Antonio, Zachary and Nicholas … You got a big job up there.”

Lorenzo’s friend, Stephen Daly, paid tribute to him in a YouTube video. He wrote in the description of the video, “From September 21st-March 9th 2020 we were blessed to have Lorenzo Brino’s Beautiful soul in our lives. The amount of people he touched with his character and charm was unbelievable. He was a loyal friend and an amazing brother. He would always get my back no matter what. Rest In Peace Brother.”
