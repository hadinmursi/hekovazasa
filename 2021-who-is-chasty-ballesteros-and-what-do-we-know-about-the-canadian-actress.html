Who is Chasty Ballesteros and What Do We Know About The Canadian Actress?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Ever seen the American political thriller series, The Newsroom? If you have then you’d certainly know Chasty Ballesteros who portrayed the role of Tea, the bartender at Hang Chews, the karaoke bar frequented by the News Night Staff. Aside from the series, the actress can also be remembered for her role in other movies such as Sons of Anarchy, Smallville, The Ranch, and The Big Bang Theory, among others.

Since the inception of her career in 2009, Chasty Ballesteros has gone on to appear in a good number of blockbuster and thriller movies and TV shows. Before acting, the actress had previously worked as a hairdresser before she eventually made a career switch in 2009. Join us as we bring you interesting facts about the actress below.

Who Exactly is Chasty Ballesteros?

She is a prolific Canadian actress of Filipino descent. Born as Chasty Rose Ballesteros on the 3rd day of January 1981, the actress is a native of Vancouver, Canada. She was raised by her parents whose identities are not known. She possesses Canadian nationality and belongs to Filipino descent.

Chasty grew up in Winnipeg province of Manitoba where she completed her basic education. After she graduated from high school, the actress moved over to Vancouver where she pursued a career as a hairstylist before going into modelling and then acting.

Career Achievements

At the initial stages of her acting career, Chasty made her first film appearance in 2009, playing the role of Britt in Encounter with Danger. Later in the same year, she also made guest appearances in TV shows like Supernatural, The Guard, and Smallville. In the years that followed, Chasty starred in a plethora of films including Final Destination 5 (2011), Three of a Kind (2012), Not Another Celebrity Movie (2013), American Dream: The True Story (2014), The Funhouse Massacre (2015), Vigilante Diaries (2016), and The Mummy (2017).

Also, in the course of her career which has spanned over a decade, the actress has equally featured in notable TV shows including Rules of Engagement, The Newsroom, Two and a Half Men, Hollywood Heights, The Girl’s Guide to Depravity, Revenge, Modern Family, The Ranch, Into the Dark, and the 2019 series, The Big Bang Theory, etc.

Does Chasty Ballesteros Have a Boyfriend?

Regarding her personal life, Chasty Ballesteros has had a fair share of romantic relationships; nevertheless, she has never made it down the aisle. She has been in a few relationships, yet decides to keep the details of her relationships and affairs under the radar. According to her Instagram page, the Canadian movie star has been in a relationship with a professional cyclist named Curtis Keene.

While nothing much is known about Curtis and when their relationship began, it is already known that the lovebirds have been together for a long while. However, Curtis reportedly popped the big question to Chasty in January 2016 while on a romantic helicopter ride in Santa Monica, California. Guess what? She said Yes!

Since then, nothing much has been heard about their relationship. Hence, it is not known if these two have exchanged marital vows or not. That notwithstanding, the couple has been living happily ever after with their dog in Los Angeles, California. Amazingly, Chasty always shares their happy moments with her fans on social media.

Chasty Ballesteros’ Net Worth

Chasty Ballesteros has won the heart of her many fans with her jaw-dropping performances in movies and TV shows. She has equally garnered both fame and the wealth that comes along with it. That said, the actress has been living her best life with a net worth currently estimated at $2 million. She made her wealth from her acting career and other business endeavours. And with the way her career is going, her net worth is set to increase in the future.

Social Media Presence and Other Interests

In addition to her acting career, Chasty Ballesteros has equally garnered fame through her social media presence. She is very active on different social media platforms where she connects with her numerous fans across the world. So far, the actress has amassed over 18 thousand followers on Instagram, with the handle @chastyballesteros. She also has several other thousand fans on Twitter and Facebook.

Ballesteros is also a beauty enthusiast and has previously worked as a hairstylist before her acting career began.

Chasty Ballesteros’ Body Measurements

The Canadian actress is not the tallest out there, but with a height of 5 feet 6 inches she won’t certainly be lost in a crowd. With a body weight of 55 kg (122 pounds), the actress rocks impeccable body measurements (bust-waist-hips) of 36-25-37 inches. More so, she also wears a bra of size 34B, a dress of size 6 (US), and a shoe of 8.5 (US). Her other distinctive features include her black hair and a pair of dark-brown eyes.
