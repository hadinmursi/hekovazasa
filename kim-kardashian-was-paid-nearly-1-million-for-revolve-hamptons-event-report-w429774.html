Kim Kardashian Was Paid Nearly $1 Million for Revolve Hamptons Event: Report

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Whole new meaning to being paid to show up. Kim Kardashian was reportedly paid nearly $1 million to host a bash for bloggers at the Revolve Hamptons House on Saturday, July 16. According to Page Six, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star earned $700,000 to mingle with style influencers. 

According to the site, Kardashian posed and took selfies with bloggers for just over an hour. The social media maven, who has ranked in $160 million from her mobile app alone, also reportedly had with her 25 bodyguards.

According to Elle, who was on hand to interview the E! personality at the bash, they had to “make it through two sets of bouncers to speak with the postmodern Cleopatra.”

During their chat, they talked about everything from Kardashian’s kids, North and Saint West, to how she runs everything by her husband, Kanye West, including her makeup tutorials.

As previously reported, the 35-year-old landed the No. 42 spot on Forbes’ Celebrity 100 list after she earned $51 million this past year, 40 percent of which came from her mobile game.
