The Truth About Taylor Swift's Relationship With Sophie Turner

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, September 9, 2024

One thing musician Taylor Swift and actress Sophie Turner have in common is singer Joe Jonas — he is Swift's ex-boyfriend and Turner's now husband. However, fans can't help but wonder where the two ladies stand when it comes to each other.

Today, we're taking a look at just what their relationship is like and if the two are friends or enemies. From how their paths cross to whether they like each other's work — keep on scrolling to find out!

Related: The Truth About Andy Cohen And Taylor Swift's Complicated Relationship

8 Taylor Swift And Joe Jonas Dated In 2008

Let's start off with the fact that musicians Taylor Swift and Joe Jonas dated from June until October 2008. As most fans know, their breakup was quite the topic, since Joe broke up with Taylor in a 27-second phone call. Here's what Joe later on revealed about the phone conversation:

"For those who have expressed concern over the '27 second' phone call, I called to discuss feelings with the other person. Those feelings were obviously not well received. I did not end the conversation. Someone else did. Phone calls can only last as long as the person on the other end of the line is willing to talk."

7 Sophie Turner Started Dating Joe Jonas In 2016

Joe Jonas started dating Game of Thrones star Sophie Turner in October 2016 and a year later the couple got engaged. The two starts tied the knot in a Las Vegas chapel wedding on May 1, 2019, after which they decided to have another, more extravagant, wedding in Paris, France on June 29, 2019.

6 Taylor Swift And Sophie Turner Appeared Together On 'The Graham Norton Show'

Of course, no one ever expected Taylor Swift and Sophie Turner to bond, but it certainly seems as if the two ladies actually like each other. In 2019, both of them attended The Graham Norton Show, and it certainly seemed as if there was no bad blood between the two.

5 Taylor Swift Wrote The Song 'Mr. Perfectly Fine' About Joe Jonas

It's no secret that Taylor Swift often writes songs about her partners, so it's certainly not surprising that fans seem to thing she also wrote one about Joe Jonas. In particular, the song "Mr. Perfectly Fine" seems to be about the Jonas Brother, and in the chorus of it, Swift is certainly roasting the singer:

"Hello Mr. Perfectly fine/How's your heart after breaking mine? / Mr. Always at the right place at the right time, baby / Hello Mr. Casually cruel / Mr. Everything revolves around you / I've been Miss Misery since your goodbye / And you're Mr. Perfectly fine."

4 Sophie Turner Shared The Song On Her Instagram Stories

Those who follow Sophie Turner on Instagram surely already know that the Queen in the North is a huge Swiftie. In fact, Turner didn't even care that Taylor Swift is roasting her husband Joe Jonas in "Mr. Perfectly Fine" — she actually shared on Instagram stories that she is listening to the song, and in addition to that, she wrote "It’s not NOT a bop @TaylorSwift."

Of course, this didn't go unnoticed by Taylor Swift, who responded to the story by reposting it and adding "Forever bending the knee for the [queen] of the north."

3 Taylor Swift Sent Joe And Sophie's Daughter A Gift

Swifties get to find out a lot about Taylor Swift's life through her songs, and one detail that didn't go unnoticed is the fact that in her song "Invisible String" she sings, "Cold was the steel of my axe to grind / For the boys who broke my heart / Now I send their babies presents." Of course, fans were quick to conclude that she is talking about Joe Jonas, as his wife Sophie Turner gave birth to their daughter Willa in July 2020.

2 Sophie Turner Praised Taylor Swift's 10-Minute Version Of 'All Too Well'

This fall Taylor Swift released a 10-minute version of the song "All Too Well" from her album Red. The song has been a fan-favorite for years, and it seems as if Sophie — just like the rest of Taylor Swift's fandom — was not ready for the full version. When the song and music video came out, the Game of Thornes star once again decided to give Taylor Swift a shoutout on her Instagram story by writing "I'm not fine at all @TaylorSwift."

1 Sophie Turner And Joe Jonas Attended Taylor Swift's 'SNL' Performance

And finally, we're wrapping the list up with the fact that Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas attended Taylor Swift's "All Too Well" performance on Saturday Night Live this past November. While Joe Jonas and Taylor Swift certainly didn't end things on a good note back in 2008, it definitely seems as if they have managed to move past that. After all, Joe's wife Sophie Turner certainly seems to be a huge fan of his ex.
