Joel Kinnaman Net Worth: How rich is Joel Kinnaman?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Swedish-born actor, Joel Kinnaman has an estimated net worth of around $14 million. Considering that he entered the industry when he was just a child, it is very understandable that he has been able to rake in such a great amount of wealth during his career.

According to sources, he made most of his wealth when he entered the United States movie industry. With the help of an American agent he hired to help extend his career, he made an international film debut in the United States when he starred in the thriller film The Darkest Hour. 

He became popular amongst American film producers and fans from 2011 to 2014 when he starred in the AMC tv series dubbed The Killing. He made his Hollywood debut with the film Safe House and Lola Versus. 

One of his most notable roles is Rick Flag in the Warner Bros. film adaptation of DC Comics anti-hero team Suicide Squad. He appeared in the sequel The Suicide Squad (2021) directed by James Gunn.

Kinnaman also starred in the political drama series House of Cards as Governor Will Conway in Seasons 4 and 5. In 2019, he played the role of Erik in Hanna, the 2011 adaptation film of the same name. He also played Andrea Di Stefano in The Informer that same year.
