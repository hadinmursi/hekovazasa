Sharon Osbourne Posts Naked Selfie to Support Kim Kardashian: NSFW Photo

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Damn! Sharon Osbourne posted a naked mirror selfie à la Kim Kardashian on Twitter on Wednesday, March 9. The Talk cohost, 63, shared the risqué snap to support the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, who has been the topic of much debate since she posted the original shot on Monday, March 7.

“@kimkardashian you inspired me! #liberated #thetalk,” Ozzy Osbourne’s wife tweeted along with the photo, in which black bars censor her NSFW parts.

Sharon’s tweet comes after Kardashian, 35, defended herself in a blog post on Tuesday amid criticism from stars including Bette Midler and Chloë Grace Moretz.

“I don’t do drugs, I hardly drink, I’ve never committed a crime — and yet I’m a bad role model for being proud of my body? … I am empowered by my body. I am empowered by my sexuality. I am empowered by feeling comfortable in my skin. I am empowered by showing the world my flaws and not being afraid of what anyone is going to say about me. And I hope that through this platform I have been given, I can encourage the same empowerment for girls and women all over the world,” Kardashian wrote in her open letter, where she also addressed her 2007 sex tape with ex-boyfriend Ray J.

“I lived through the embarrassment and fear, and decided to say who cares, do better, move on. I shouldn’t have to constantly be on the defense, listing off my accomplishments just to prove that I am more than something that happened 13 years ago,” she added, referring to when the video was filmed in 2003. “Let’s move on already. I have.”
