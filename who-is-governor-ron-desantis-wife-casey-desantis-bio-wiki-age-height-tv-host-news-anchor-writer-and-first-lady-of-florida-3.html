Casey DeSantis Tv Host, News Anchor, and Writer

Earlier in her career, she hosted Golf Channel programs On The Tee and PGA Tour Today. She was also a local newscaster and anchor for WJXTand held several positions, including general assignment reporter, morning anchor, and police reporter. She also did special reports, including for CNN. In 2014, DeSantis was a creator-moderator for The Chat, an hour-long round table panel discussion talk show simulcast on NBC and ABC.

She hosts the daily NBC talk show First Coast Living as well as the weekly The American Dream program about entrepreneurs. She wrote and produced the television documentary Champion, The JT Townsend Story about high school football player J.T. Townsend. She campaigned for her husband and plays a leading role in his administration. In February, she established the First Lady’s Medal for Courage Commitment and service. She participated in a Recovery Redfish Release to address the devastating impacts of red tide.
