Kendra Wilkinson Snapchats Her First Botox Procedure

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, September 7, 2024

Stick to it! Kendra Wilkinson got Botox for the very first time and documented her experience in a Thursday, August 25, Snapchat story.

“So I’m finally giving in, I’m doing Botox for the first time ever, all because of Jessica,” the Kendra on Top star, 31, said, placing the blame on fellow former Playboy model Jessica Hall. “I definitely need it, though,” she added, pointing at faint lines above her nose, mouth and eyes.

While in the chair of Dr. Alex Eshaghian in Encino, California, the former Girls Next Door star — who shares Hank, 6, and Alijah, 2, with husband Hank Baskett — answered a questionnaire about her skin and said she thinks it is holding up well considering her Playmate past. “It’s not aging as much as I thought it would at this time,” she said. “I thought it would because of all the drugs I’ve done.”

Though she may have liked to party in her younger days, the doctor told Wilkinson that she couldn’t consume alcohol for 24 hours. But luckily, that’s about as bad as the appointment got. The reality personality was able to power through the Botox process … even though she has a fear of needles. “That was nothing,” Wilkinson noted as the doctor pricked her forehead. “This is nothing.” 

Wilkinson’s appointment went by pretty fast, and she couldn’t have been happier with the results (though she did flip the middle finger to her friend Hall!). “I got Botox, guys,” the Sliding Into Home author shrieked. “I’m so happy right now!”
