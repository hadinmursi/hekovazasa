Taylor Swifts Ex Joe Alwyn Is Worried About Her New Relationship With Travis Kelce

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift's ex-boyfriend Joe Alwyn doesn't seem impressed with her new romance with Travis Kelce, as he was always more private in their relationship.
  • Travis is more than happy to showcase his relationship with Taylor, which could be a turn on for Taylor given her notoriety and desire for freedom.
  • Hemmings believes that Taylor is making up for lost time with Travis, as rumors suggest Joe was unwilling to settle down after six years together. Hemmings sees Travis as a positive force in Taylor's life.

Swifties may be thrilled to see Taylor Swift in a new romance with Travis Kelce, given all of the attention surrounding their relationship status. However, there’s one person who doesn’t appear impressed – her ex-boyfriend Joe Alwyn.

Speaking to The Sun, psychologist Jo Hemmings analyzed the differences between Taylor’s relationships with Travis and Kelce. She points out that while Travis is more than happy to showcase their romance, Joe was always more private, which could signal that he as well as loved ones in Taylor’s life are concerned about the fat-pace of her new romance with Travis.

Taylor Is Acting Differently In Her Relationship With Travis

"Joe seemed to rush Taylor in and out of events,” Hemmings explained. “It was almost as if he was embarrassed to be with her.”

While this may be a red flag, it could also be a turn on for Taylor, as having a boyfriend who’s more secure with a public presence may be easier given her notoriety. "Travis is only too happy to showcase Taylor,” the expert continued. "Now she has her first real taste of freedom in a long time and with a boyfriend who is much more secure in himself.”

Hemmings also had an explanation for why things seem to be moving so fast in Travis and Taylor’s relationship – she’s “making up for lost time,” the expert explained, referencing rumors that Joe was unwilling to settle down with Taylor after six years together.

"This is genuine and she is showing mature authenticity,” Hemming said, emphasizing her belief that Travis is a positive force in Taylor’s life. "At the end of the day, Taylor and Travis have nothing to hide. This is all healthy and good for her.”

Though Taylor may seem head-over-heels in her new relationship, there’s been various concerns over it, including from Travis’ family, who are apparently worried about the newfound attention Travis is receiving and whether it could pose a security risk.

Travis’ Loved Ones Are Worried About Their Relationship

"It's certainly been weird, the level that it is now," Jason, also a football star, told NBC Sports. "On one hand, I'm happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he's excited about, that he is genuine about. But there's another end of it where it's like, ‘Man, this is a lot.'"

"[This] is another level of stardom that typically football players don't deal with,” he explained.

"So, on one hand, really, really happy for my brother and where he's at in his current situation with Taylor," Jason continued. "But on the other hand, there's some alarms sometimes with how over-in-pursuit people can be."

All the publicity surrounding his relationship with Taylor apparently prompted Travis to invest in a new $6 million mansion with enhanced security features.

Moreover, there have also been concerns from Travis’ ex-girlfriends, including Maya Benberry, who issued a statement to Daily Mail claiming Travis cheated on her and cautioning Taylor to be careful.
