Who is Kim Matula? Here are Facts You Need To Know About The Actress

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 1, 2024

A relatively fresh-to-screen face, Kim Matula is presumably living out her dreams. The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) alum discovered her flair for acting at an early age and was quite certain of her career path before college. Yet another proof that schooling doesn’t always correlate with success, Matula later quit school to fully pursue her passion. Here are answers to frequently asked questions about the beautiful and talented actress.

Who Exactly is Kim Matula?

Kim Matula is a Texan who was welcomed into the world on August 23, 1988. The actress and her sister Katherine were born to Karin and Kim Matula. Kim is of mixed ethnicity as she has Norwegian roots.

Matula’s inclination towards the performing arts was evident from a young age. Although there is a scarcity of information about her early life and elementary education, it is common knowledge that the actress was certain of her career path from an early age and began assiduously working towards achieving her dreams.

For her tertiary education, Kim Matula studied film at the University of Texas. It was while in college that she ventured into acting and later ditched her studies to fully pursue a career in the entertainment industry.

Professional Accomplishments

Kim Matula made her acting debut on the big screen in the year 2006 after landing a role as Megan in Savage Spirit. Her outstanding performance in the movie paved way for other subsequent roles. She has since followed up with notable roles such as Tara in the 2008 movie Queen Sized, Sabrina in Spilt Milk (2010), Donna in Dawn Patrol, and Lacey in Maybe Someday. She has also appeared in short films such as Crown thy Good (2010), Cool Wheels (2010), and Broken Strings (2014). More recently, Kim appeared as Jeri-Lynn in the 2019 film Fighting with My Family.

Kim Matula is an actress who shines more on the small screen. She got her breakout role as Hope Logan in The Bold and the Beautiful. Between 2010 and 2016, she had both guest and recurring roles in the CBS series. Matula’s outstanding performance in the daytime soap earned her a Daytime Emmy Award nomination. In 2016, she also landed a major role as Tiffany in the series UnReal. Some of Kim’s other TV credits include How I Met Your Mother (2009), The Defenders (2011), and Almost Royal (2014).

Kim Matula’s Love Life

Many fans have been curious about Kim Matula’s relationship status since she came into the limelight. However, the actress is one of those stars who prefer to keep their love lives on a low key. Regardless, it is no secret that the beautiful and talented actress is not yet married and is sans kids.

Despite the mystery shrouding her dating history, Matula has been romantically linked to some of her colleagues including Darin Brooks, Scott Clifton, and Ashleigh Brewer. But details regarding these relationships have not been disclosed nor were they confirmed.

Currently, she is speculated to be in a relationship with actor Ben Goldberg but it has also not been confirmed by either party. However, judging by how lovey-dovey Kim and Ben have been in public and on social media, they are presumed a perfect match and fans are hopeful that their relationship will end happily ever after.

Net Worth

Though relatively a fresh-to-screen face, Kim Matula has so far accumulated impressive credits both on the small and big screens. While she is perhaps yet to get to the peak of her career, Matula’s numerous efforts have been financially rewarding. Her exact salary has not been disclosed but she currently enjoys a decent net worth that is pegged at about $2 million, a figure that is expected to increase at a prolific rate in the near future.

She Loves Animals

Animals have a special place in Matula’s heart and home. She nurtures a cat as a pet and considers the feline an integral part of her family.

Social Media Presence

Kim Matula has an active social media life. While she may not be present on all popular social networking platforms, the actress has amassed a strong fanbase on Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram.

Hobbies

When she is not working, Kim Matula loves to unwind through a variety of fun activities. In addition to spending quality time with her family, the B&B alum enjoys going on safari trips.

Height

Kim Matula has impressive physical features. The blonde-haired actress stands at an average height of 5 feet 5 inches, complemented by weight of about 56 kg. Her other body measurements are bust – 35 inches, waist – 24 inches, and hips – 36 inches.
