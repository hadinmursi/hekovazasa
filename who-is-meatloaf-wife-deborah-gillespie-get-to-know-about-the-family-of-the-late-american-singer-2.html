Who Is Meatloaf Wife Deborah Gillespie? Get To Know About The Family of The Late American Singer

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Deborah Gillespie, Meatloaf’s wife was just beside him before he took his last breath. Get to know details on the late singer’s spouse here.

Deborah Gillespie was married to Meatloaf aka Michael Lee Aday. Her husband, an American singer has died on January 20, 2022, at the age of 74.

Meatloaf was an extremely popular singer best known for his wide-ranging vocals. His Bat Out of Hell trilogy has been sold over 65 million.

Besides, music, the iconic singer also appeared in over 50 movies. His film roles include Fight Club, The Rocky Horror Picture Show, and others.

A Facebook post had stated that the musician’s wife was by his side before he took his last breath. Get to know details on his devoted wife, Deborah.

Who Is Meatloaf Wife Deborah Gillespie?

Meatloaf aka Michael Lee Aday was survived by his wife Deborah Gillespie. The couple has been married since 2007.

Deborah is reportedly from Canada. She is his second wife before, Aday was married to Leslie Aday from (1971 to 2001).

The pair split their time between Los Angeles and Austin, Texas, where the rock star was born.

At the time of Michael’s death, Deborah was by her side as announced through a FB post. However, his cause of death has not been mentioned.

The post reads: “Our hearts are broken to announce that the incomparable Meat Loaf passed away tonight with his wife Deborah by his side.”

According to Deborah’s IMDB profile, she has starred in Meatloaf’s two documentaries;  Meat Loaf: Live with the Melbourne Symphony Orchestra (2004) and Meat Loaf: In Search of Paradise (2007).

Meet Meatloaf & Deborah Gillespie Children

Meatloaf aka Michael Lee Aday and his wife Deborah Gillespie had no children together.

However, Deborah is stepmother to Aday’s children; Amanda and Pearl Aday, from his first wife.

The FB statement reads: “Daughters Pearl and Amanda and close friends have been with him throughout the last 24 hours.”

Meatloaf’s two beautiful daughters have become celebrities in their own right.

Pearl is now a singer in New York, she used to be a member of Neverland Express in the 90s. In fact, she has sung alongside her iconic father.

As of now, Pearl is 47 years old and she is still into music. Moreover, she is also the lead singer of a metal band called, Mötley Crüe.

Meatloaf’s biological daughter Amanda has found herself rocking in the acting industry. She is known as the Dora Mae Dreifuss in HBO’s Carnivàle.

Besides, Amanda has been part of films like; South Dakota, Madtown, and The Trials of Cate McCall.

Deborah Gillespie Age & Wikipedia

Deborah Gillespie’s age is probably in the sixties. As of now, we lack details related to her birthday as she does not have a Wikipedia bio.
