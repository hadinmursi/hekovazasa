Who is Dan Reynolds? Dan Reynolds Biography and Wiki

Dan Reynolds is an American singer, songwriter and record producer who is a native of Las Vegas, Nevada. He is well known as the front-man for the Imagine Dragons pop-rock band that also features Ben Mckee, Wayne Sermon, and Daniel Platzman. His band is popular for releasing hits like Radioactive, On Top of the World, Believer among others you will find listed.

Dan Reynolds Disease

In 2015 during Imagine Dragons Smoke + Mirrors tour, its lead singer announced that he suffers from ankylosing spondylitis, a type of arthritis that is characterized by long term inflammation of the spine’s joints. He has partnered with organizations like ‘ThisASLife’ to raise awareness about ankylosing spondylitis.

Besides, he also struggles with ulcerative colitis since he was 21 years old. The two conditions have depressed him over the years but he is now recuperating with the help of a therapist. Many songs he has written as a song writer were inspired by his depression.

Imagine Dragons Members

Imagine Dragons is a famous rock band that was formed in the year 2010 by Wayne Sermon and his friends Dan Reynolds, Ben McKee, and Daniel Platzman. The latter is the band’s drummer while Sermon is the bassist. The only remaining original member of the band, Reynolds, is the key vocalist.

The band’s debut album is titled Night Visions and was released in September 2012. It reached #2 on the Billboard 200 chart and won the 2014 Billboard Music Award for Best Rock Album.
