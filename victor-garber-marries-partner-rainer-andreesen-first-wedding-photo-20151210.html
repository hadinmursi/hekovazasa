Victor Garber Marries Partner Rainer Andreesen: First Wedding Photo!

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

He's hitched! Victor Garber married his longtime artist boyfriend, Rainer Andreesen, on Saturday, Oct. 10.

Andreesen, 52, announced the news with a sweet Instagram photo of the newlyweds. "YES WE DID!" he captioned the snapshot. #justgotmarried #elopedintofino #canada @therealvictorgarber #after16years."

The couple — who began dating in 1999 — coordinated in black tuxes and gray ties for their big day. They smiled big for the camera and wrapped their arms around each other.

The Legends of Tomorrow actor, 66 — who officiated former Alias costar Jennifer Garner's 2005 nuptials to Ben Affleck — came out as gay in April 2012.

"My companion Rainer Andreesen and I have been together almost 13 years in Greenwich Village. We both love New York," he told Canada's Forever Young at the time.

In January 2013, Garber brought the fellow Canadian to the SAG Awards. "I don't really talk about it but everybody knows," he told Greg in Hollywood blogger Greg Hernandez.
