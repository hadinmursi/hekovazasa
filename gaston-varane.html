Gaston Varane is the father of Raphael Varane, a renowned football player who plays as a centre-back for one of the most prestigious clubs in the world called Real Madrid and for the national team of France. 

Gaston hails from North of Martinique Island, Le Morne-Rouge. He emigrated with his spouse Annie Varane in small-town Hellemmes-Lille in France in 1976.

About Raphael Varane:

He is a professional football player from France who plays for the club Real Madrid and the national team of France as a Centre-Back. 

He has won over 18 major honors, including three titles of La Liga, four UEFA Champions League, one Copa del Rey, and four FIFA Club world cup. 

He was born on the 25th of April 1993 in Lille, France, to his father, Gaston Varane, and mother, Annie Varane. His mother was brought up in Saint-Amand-Les-Eaux, whereas his father hails from Le Morne-Rouge. 

He started playing football from a very young age with his father. And later, he played football with his regional football teams and his father. He began his sports journey from rugby, and later, he got interested in football.
