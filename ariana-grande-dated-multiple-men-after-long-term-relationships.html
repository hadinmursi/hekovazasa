Ariana Grande Dated Multiple Men Fresh Out Of Long Term Relationships, Here's Why

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Highlights

  • Ariana Grande has a pattern of quickly moving on to new relationships after long-term commitments.
  • Experts suggest that Grande's dating preferences may be influenced by her partners' recent breakups.
  • Grande's dating history includes allegations of infidelity.

Ariana Grande's long list of high-profile and controversial relationships always piques the curiosity of the public. Her dating preferences, particularly her tendency to enter into new relationships shortly after the end of long-term commitments, led to intense criticism.

While Grande's fans may argue that she simply moves on easily or that it serves as her coping mechanism after a breakup, besides her music, experts have a different perspective on the pop sensation's romantic inclinations.

Ariana Grande's Low Self-Esteem May Be Why She Gets Into Relationships With Committed Men

Relationship coaches Hayley Quinn and Kezia Noble, were interviewed by the Daily Mail to share their insights about Ariana Grande's dating history. Noble stated that Grande's dating pattern might be influenced by the fact that men who have recently ended relationships are often more predisposed to committing to a new one.

She said that the pop star might be drawn to these men because they are more likely to seek emotional solace and commitment after their previous breakups.

Noble also explained that individuals coming out of relationships tend to be emotionally vulnerable and are often eager to find someone to replace their ex-partners, potentially leading to quicker transitions into new relationships as a means of coping with post-breakup pain and loneliness.

Quinn added that despite external appearances, even those who seemingly have everything may not necessarily possess high self-esteem. According to Quinn, Grande may not hold particularly high standards for her partners and could be prone to rushing into relationships as soon as interest is expressed.

Quinn also noted that some individuals, including Grande, may enter into relationships hastily due to underlying feelings of insecurity about being alone. "People can rush into relationships when, deep down, they feel insecure about being alone. They can become addicted to the exciting first stages of romance, and simply overlook any red flags in their new partner."

She added, "This means if someone's already in a relationship, it might be viewed as an exciting part of the "chase" to finally being able to be together; rather than a warning sign about their trustworthiness."

In the long run, Quinn emphasized that lasting relationships must navigate periods of routine and require commitment even as the initial excitement wanes. She stressed that building enduring relationships hinges on shared values, mutual relationship goals, and respect for one another. Relying on excitement and uncertainty to maintain romantic connections often results in short-lived relationships.

Instead, Quinn argued that chasing the "high" of initial excitement with a new love interest can hinder the development of genuine emotional intimacy and connection.

Ariana Grande Allegedly Dated Big Sean And Mac Miller While They Were Still In Relationships

Ariana Grande's dating history has not been without its fair share of controversy and allegations of infidelity. This isn't the first time Grande was accused of being a serial home-wrecker due to her involvement with someone newly single.

The first accusation was made by her ex, Jai Brooks in 2013 when he claimed Grande cheated on him with The Wanted's Nathan Sykes. Her relationship with Sykes was short-lived, and the very next year, she began dating Big Sean, even though he was engaged to the late Glee star, Naya Rivera.
