Pregnant Kim Kardashian Works Bump in Tight White Dress, Heels

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Hey, baby! Pregnant Kim Kardashian, who’s expecting her second child (and first son!) with husband Kanye West, was snapped running errands in L.A. on Monday, July 27, wearing a bump-hugging ensemble and sky-high sandals—all before sending a sweet message to her unborn son.

Despite being well into her second trimester, the reality star, 34, could not resist finishing her skintight white dress and peach Margiela coat with nude Manolo Blahnik ankle-strap sandals. And yet, despite the heels’ height and her blossoming bump, the fashionista strolled with ease.
