OJ Simpson’s defence attorney during his trial for the murder of his wife, Johnnie L Cochran Jr. was born on October 2, 1937, in Shreveport, Louisiana, USA. He is most known for representing Simpson during his trial for the death of his wife. He has also represented a number of well-known figures in the entertainment business, and all of his efforts contributed to his net worth reaching the level it was at the time of his death in 2005.

What was Johnnie Cochran’s net worth? As of mid-2016, sources indicate that he had a net worth of $8 million, which he derived mostly from a successful career as a trial lawyer. He has collaborated with a number of high-profile individuals, including Jim Brown, Snoop Dogg, Michael Jackson, and Geronimo Pratt, among others. He was well-known for his courtroom prowess as well as his opposition to police brutality and other forms of injustice. All of these factors contributed to his wealth’s status.
