What Does Hella Studious Mean? TikTok Meaning and urban Dictionary

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

The phrase Hella Studious is going viral on platforms like TikTok and Reddit, and people are curious to know what does it mean.

Tiktok has been a platform where phrases, songs, and dance moves are viral now and then. While in Reddit, different issues and topics are discussed and brought into the limelight.

In recent, Hella Studious has been used a lot by TikTok users, due to which it has come to trend. Similarly, in Reddit, many people are using it for humorous videos.

What Does Hella Studious Mean? TikTok Meaning Explored

The Hella Studious is used to denote a studious person and is used by the users in Tiktok to show a person studying sincerely and writing notes in the class. Similarly, a Youtube video which had a compilation of the TikTok videos, showed how users were using the phrase on Tiktok.

Some users in the Tiktok are using it to make fun of the person who is doing something else, then studying at the class. While some people are also using it to show the people who are faking themselves as a studious student.

Many people in TikTok are using the phrase according to the perspective they want to show on the platform. Similarly, some are showing their creativity on a whole different level on the phrase in Tiktok.

The trend is going strong on the platform with many videos uploaded from the users, and it seems to be fo trend for the coming days.

Hella Studious Mean In Urban Dictionary

Hella Studious means hell smart or hard worker in Urban Dictionary. It is used to denote the studious nature of the people.

It is used in the sentence to denote if someone is working hard on their studies. If you want to say someone is studying hard nowadays, then you can use hella studious to denote studious nature.
