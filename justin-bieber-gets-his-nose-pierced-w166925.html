Justin Bieber Gets His Nose Pierced

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Double take! Justin Bieber shared a photo of himself via Instagram on Friday, March 11, that reveals his newly pierced nose.

In the selfie, a shirtless Bieber, 22, hangs out in bed and gives the camera a blank stare. His nostril on one side is pierced with a small stud.

Biebs posted and deleted another snap of his new nose bling on Friday, which gave an even closer look at the shiny stud dotting his face. In addition to his latest piercing, the platinum-haired playboy also has both of his earlobes pierced along with numerous tattoos.

Bieber, who frequently shares photos of himself in bed, explained to GQ last month that he suffers from insomnia and takes medication. “I’m not getting restful sleep, so during the day I need [Adderall] to concentrate because I’m not getting the proper—you know, when you sleep, your body creates endorphins, creates these things, and when you’re not getting that sleep…” he said.

The pop star has a very hectic schedule ahead after embarking on his world tour for his fourth studio album, Purpose, two days ago in Seattle. The tour will end this November in London.

The "Love Yourself" singer recently celebrated his 22nd birthday on March 1, and marked the occasion with a star-studded bash on February 27. Among the guests were his brief hookup Kourtney Kardashian, rumored love interest Hailey Baldwin and Diddy.
