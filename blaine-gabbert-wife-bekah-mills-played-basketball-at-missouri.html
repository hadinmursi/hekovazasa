Mills worked as an audit associate when she first started work at the company in 2012. 

Mills continued to work in the audit associate position for a year and nine months till the mid of 2014. She was then promoted to the high position of senior audit associate. 

Bekah worked as a senior audit manager for around three years, from July 2014 to June 2017.  

Currently, she has been working at KPMG since the month of July 2017. She is mainly involved in organizing and managing the internal audits based on the plans.

She has been in the position for more than five years. She has the skills such as financial statement auditing and analysis, SEC filings, US GAAP, financial reporting, external audit, and CPA.
